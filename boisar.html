overview

Happinest, Avadi is located off Poonamallee Avadi High Road. The project is spread Over approximately 5 hectares and comprises of 1268 apartments, 1 & 2 BHK.

At Happinest you can be assured of a comfortable and carefully designed home. With well-ventilated rooms, large balconies and verandahs, you can be sure of enjoying a healthy life.

From vast playgrounds for children to run around, to parks for the elders to relax, large tree-lined spaces and a spacious community area to interact in, it’s like one big family enjoying a clean, green and close-knit existence.
